WE ARE UBA. THE LEADING STUDENT BUSINESS ASSOCIATION AT NYU SHANGHAI

We serve to educate and empower students who are looking to excel in business and any related fields. We accomplish our mission through student-run events, workshops, and networking opportunities.

What to expect from UBA?

Workshop

Regular events that encourage and guide members in career progress through building professional self-branding as well as valuable advice from guest speakers in various industries.

Mentorship

Mentor-Mentee pairings belong to different UBA ‘Families’ which are led by different E-Board members. This allows a more interconnected and interactive experience, providing opportunities for all club members to bond.

Networking

Networking opportunities in both our Workshops and Mentorships, especially through our Speaker Series, and more!
